The Strikes

Monitoring US-led strikes aganist ISIL, in Iraq and Syria.

Tweet

The US-led Campaign against ISIL consists of 40+ member country. Many countries contributed in the campaign by directly striking ISIL positions, others send troops to train local forces, and others send Military aid, Humanitarian aid and some countries contributed by Intelligence informations.

Some fighting Countries intervening in Iraq only, some in Syria only, and others intervening in both (Iraq & Syria).

Some support Countries offered Military aid, some Humanitarian aid, and others Intelligence aid.

  • Intervening in Iraq & Syria.
  • Intervening in Iraq only.
  • Intervening in Syria only.

Although the severe striking against ISIL forces/facilities in Iraq & Syria, US-led coalition trying to make local fighters (Iraqi army, Peshmerga, Syrian kurdish militias) get the most benefit from the strikes effect, to gain more Influence on the ground against ISIL forces.

Strikes vary from city to another.

In this Graph the cities divided by number of strikes, number of targets bombed, and country.

  • X-axis: City
  • Y-axis: Number of strikes per city
  • Radius of the circle represents the total of targets bombed per city

The large area of ISIL operations in Iraq and Syria lead to a massive numbers/categories of targets that could be bombed by US-led Coalition.

The coalition strikes ISIL to 1) Undermines ISIL's power and 2) To support local forces (Iraqi Army, Kurdish militias) which lead to a diversity in types of targets

Although the strikes targeting ISIL facilities/forces in Iraq and Syria mainly, but many civilians died due to the coalition airstrikes.

The numbers are from start of the strikes till 31th March 2015, and the total numbers of civilian casualties may increase.
